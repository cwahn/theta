## Release v0.1.0-alpha.56

- **Refactor: Eliminate unnecessary `#[allow(clippy::...)]` annotations** — Critically analyzed all crate-level and item-level allow annotations across the workspace. Removed `#![allow(clippy::unused_async)]` from `theta/src/lib.rs` by converting the three `__default_*` trait helper functions (`__default_supervise`, `__default_on_restart`, `__default_on_exit`) from `async fn` to `fn` returning `impl Future` via `std::future::ready(...)`. Removed `async` from `cleanup_children` (no awaits in body, changed to `fn(&self)`) and `process_forward` in `peer.rs` (also no awaits). Annotated remaining `cast_possible_truncation` suppression sites in `theta-macros` with `FIXME` comments explaining the planned ad-hoc polymorphism redesign.

- `theta@0.1.0-alpha.56`
